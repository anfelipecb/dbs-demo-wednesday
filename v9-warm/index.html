The Method

Randomized controlled trial

Caseworkers randomly assigned access to Nexo. Outcomes tracked through ADE administrative records.

Treatment
Nexo AI Copilot
Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recommendations, and admin automation. Human discretion retained.
Control
Standard Workflow
Same jobseekers, same outcome tracking — no AI access. Isolating the causal effect.
