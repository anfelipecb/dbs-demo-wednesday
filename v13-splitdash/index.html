Nexo

AI copilot for caseworkers. Scroll to explore the data ↓

The Scale

38,844 workers placed across 20 localities

The ADE is Bogota's frontline employment office. Every placement — from registration to follow-up — flows through a human caseworker.

The Pipeline

Five stages. 4.4 hours per jobseeker.

Registration, orientation, training, matching, follow-up. Every stage demands caseworker time — the bottleneck that limits scale.

Nexo AI Capabilities

AI that amplifies every stage

CV drafting, skill profiling, job matching, admin automation. Caseworkers retain full discretion — accept, edit, or ignore.

Projected Impact

Measurable outcomes from AI assistance

Based on comparable interventions: +34% placements, 3x throughput, reduced unemployment duration, higher starting wages.

Treatment vs Control

A randomized controlled trial

Caseworkers randomly assigned to Nexo. Same jobseekers, same outcome metrics — isolating the causal effect of AI access.
