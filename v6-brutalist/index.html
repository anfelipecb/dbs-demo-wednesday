[03] METHOD

RANDOMIZED
CONTROLLED TRIAL

[TREATMENT] Nexo AI Copilot

Caseworkers assigned Nexo. Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recommendations, and admin automation. Human discretion retained at all times. Measuring: wages, placement rates, unemployment duration.

[CONTROL] Standard Workflow

Standard ADE process. Same jobseeker population, same outcome tracking. No AI access. Isolating the causal effect of AI assistance on match quality and caseworker throughput.
