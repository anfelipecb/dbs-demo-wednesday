Caseworkers are the primary resource for public employment services to help unemployed individuals find jobs. They are expected to deliver individualized, personalized support to a large and diverse pool of jobseekers. However, doing this at scale through human caseworkers alone is inherently limited.

Nexo leverages generative AI technologies to assist caseworkers in simplifying time-intensive administrative tasks. This allows them to focus on complex, non-routine work where they have the most value — management, social skills, and communication — tasks that cannot easily be replicated by AI.

Importantly, caseworkers retain full discretion to accept, edit, or ignore all suggestions. The tool encodes the best practices of top-performing caseworkers in a form that can then be delivered to other agents across the system.

The Method

A randomized controlled trial at the Agencia Distrital de Empleo, Bogota.

Treatment Arm

Caseworkers assigned Nexo AI copilot. Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recommendations, and admin automation. Human discretion retained at every step.

Control Arm

Caseworkers operate under standard ADE workflow. Same jobseeker population, same outcome tracking — isolating the causal effect of AI access on match quality.
