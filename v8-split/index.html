AI copilot for caseworkers. Scroll to explore the story ↓

The Scale

38,844 workers placed in a single year

The Agencia Distrital de Empleo is Bogota's public employment office, serving all 20 localities. Every placement flows through a caseworker.

The Pipeline

Five stages. 4.4 hours per jobseeker.

From registration to placement, each jobseeker passes through a multi-stage pipeline. Every stage demands caseworker time — the bottleneck that limits scale.

The Solution

Nexo: AI that amplifies, not replaces

Nexo drafts CVs, maps skills, ranks job matches, and automates admin tasks. Caseworkers retain full discretion — they accept, edit, or ignore every suggestion.

The Impact

Projected outcomes from the AI copilot

Based on comparable interventions in the literature, Nexo is projected to increase placement rates by 34%, cut unemployment spells, and triple caseworker throughput.

The Method

A randomized controlled trial

Caseworkers are randomly assigned access to Nexo. Outcomes tracked via ADE administrative records: wages, placement rates, unemployment duration.
