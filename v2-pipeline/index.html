Caseworker Action Path

How Nexo amplifies
every stage

The ADE's five-stage pipeline — from first contact to job placement — with AI assistance at each step. Caseworkers retain full discretion on every suggestion.

Stage 01
Registration & CV Creation
The jobseeker is registered in the national employment database. Caseworker collects personal details, work history, and documents to build their profile.
National DB Identity Work history
1
Nexo AI
Automated CV drafting
Generates a structured, employer-ready CV from the jobseeker's raw information. Caseworker reviews and edits before submission.
◆ Saves ~40 min per case
Stage 02
Occupational Orientation
In-depth profiling of the jobseeker's skills, competencies, and interests. Caseworker conducts interviews and assessments to understand fit and potential.
Skills profiling Interests Competencies
2
Nexo AI
Occupational advice & skill mapping
Surfaces relevant occupations based on profile, flags transferable skills, and suggests focus areas — drawing on best practices from top-performing caseworkers.
◆ Encodes top-caseworker playbooks
Stage 03
Training & Skills Gap
Caseworker refers jobseekers to relevant training programs, workshops, and certification courses to close identified skills gaps before job matching.
Upskilling Workshops Certifications
3
Nexo AI
Targeted training recommendations
Identifies the shortest path to employment by recommending only the training that directly closes gaps between the jobseeker's profile and target vacancies.
◆ Reduces training detours
Stage 04
Job Matching & Referral
Core matching stage. Caseworker identifies vacancies that align with the jobseeker's profile and employer requirements, then facilitates applications.
Vacancy search Employer fit Application support
4
Nexo AI
Ranked match recommendations
Generates personalized, ranked vacancy recommendations. Also assists employers with designing job postings to improve incoming match quality.
◆ 91% match accuracy
Stage 05
Placement & Follow-up
Once hired, caseworkers track outcomes — wages, retention, contract type — and follow up with both employers and workers to ensure lasting placement quality.
Wages Retention Outcome tracking
5
Nexo AI
Outcome reporting & follow-up drafts
Auto-generates follow-up communications and outcome summaries for caseworker review, reducing post-placement admin burden while maintaining documentation standards.
◆ 72% admin time reduction

Evaluation Design
Randomized Controlled Trial
Caseworkers at the ADE are randomly assigned access to Nexo. This design allows us to causally identify the effect of AI assistance on match quality, caseworker productivity, and jobseeker outcomes.
Treatment arm
Caseworkers assigned Nexo AI copilot. Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recommendations, and admin automation. Human discretion retained at all times.
Control arm
Caseworkers operate under standard ADE workflow. Same jobseeker population, same performance metrics — isolating the causal effect of AI access.
Primary outcomes
Wages, placement rates, and unemployment spell duration — drawn from ADE administrative records for every served jobseeker.
Secondary outcomes
Caseworker behavior and workload measured via purpose-built survey instrument. AI tool adoption rates and reasons for uptake or resistance.
