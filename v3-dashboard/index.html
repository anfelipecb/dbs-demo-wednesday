Evaluation Design Randomized Controlled Trial
Treatment Arm
Caseworkers assigned Nexo AI copilot. Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recommendations, and admin automation.

Human discretion retained at every step — AI suggests, caseworker decides.
Control Arm
Caseworkers operate under standard ADE workflow. Same jobseeker population, same outcome tracking — isolating the causal effect of AI access on match quality and speed.
Research Contributions 3 Novel Findings
01  Public-sector AI productivity
Extends GenAI + worker productivity evidence to a multi-party public-sector setting where the AI user (caseworker) and beneficiary (jobseeker) are distinct.
02  Allocative efficiency of PES
First experimental estimate of how AI changes resource allocation in Public Employment Services — measuring capacity expansion without added headcount.
03  Global PES modernization
Policy-relevant evidence on AI tool adoption for PES offices worldwide, including uptake barriers, training needs, and resistance patterns among caseworkers.
