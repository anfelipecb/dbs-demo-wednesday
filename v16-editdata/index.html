Caseworkers at the Agencia Distrital de Empleo are the primary resource for helping unemployed individuals find work. They deliver individualized support to a large and diverse pool of jobseekers — but doing this at scale through human caseworkers alone is inherently limited.

Nexo leverages generative AI to assist caseworkers with time-intensive administrative tasks, freeing them to focus on complex, non-routine work where human judgment matters most: social skills, communication, and management. The tool encodes best practices from top-performing caseworkers and delivers them across the system.

The Method

A randomized controlled trial at the Agencia Distrital de Empleo, Bogota.

Treatment Arm

Caseworkers assigned Nexo AI copilot. Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recommendations, and admin automation. Human discretion at every step.

Control Arm

Standard ADE workflow. Same jobseekers, same outcome tracking — no AI access. Isolating the causal effect of AI assistance.
