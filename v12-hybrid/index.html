Methodology

Randomized controlled trial

Caseworkers randomly assigned access to Nexo. Outcomes tracked via ADE records.

Treatment
Nexo AI Copilot
Full access to CV drafting, skill profiling, match recs, admin automation. Human discretion at every step.
Control
Standard Workflow
Same jobseekers, same outcomes — no AI. Isolating the causal effect of AI access.
